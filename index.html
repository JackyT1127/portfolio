Tokyo Short-Term Rental Market Analysis

June 2025

Goal: Analyze Tokyo’s short-term rental market focusing on domestic tourism trends and competitive pricing strategies.

Results: Identified key factors affecting occupancy such as pricing balance, proximity to transit and tourist spots, and host responsiveness. Highlighted high-value and high-competition areas, providing investment insights for new entrants.

Sports Nutrition Market Web Scraping Tool

April 2025

Goal: Develop a web scraper to collect pricing, product variety, and stock information across major sports nutrition retailers in Hong Kong.

Method: Built an automated Selenium-based crawler targeting iHerb, BCShop, and Decathlon, scraping over 35,000 data points in 14 days. Data stored in PostgreSQL for analysis.

Results: Delivered actionable insights on pricing gaps, brand presence, inventory trends, and marketing strategies. Proposed a roadmap for sentiment analysis and inventory alert systems.

Technical Highlights: Selenium multi-threaded scraping, database design, data validation, and future AI-driven market intelligence plans.
