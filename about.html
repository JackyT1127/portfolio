About Me

Hello, I’m Jacky!

I’m a detail-oriented data analyst with a foundation in psychology and a passion for solving complex problems through data. My background gives me a unique edge in understanding user behavior and experimental design, while my technical training has equipped me to work hands-on with tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, and statistical modeling.

I specialize in cleaning, processing, and analyzing data to uncover patterns, build reliable dashboards, and support informed decision-making. I enjoy working on technical challenges — from building data pipelines to visualizing insights — and take pride in writing clean, efficient code.

Whether it’s optimizing marketing strategy, evaluating product performance, or conducting data-driven research, I aim to bridge the gap between raw data and actionable insight through rigorous, structured analysis.
